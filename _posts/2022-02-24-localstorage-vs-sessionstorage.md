date: 2022-02-24
categories:

tags:
  - javascript
  - web
excerpt: "localStorage persists across tabs; sessionStorage is scoped to the current tab."
---

| | `localStorage` | `sessionStorage` |
|---|---|---|
| **Scope** | Persists across tabs | Scoped to the current tab |
| **Persistence** | Until explicitly cleared or browser closed | Cleared when tab is closed |
