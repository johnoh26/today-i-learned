- **URL** = Uniform Resource Locator
- Common protocols: `https:` (web), `ftp:` (file transfer), `mailto:` (email/SMTP)
- `//` after the protocol indicates the URL contains a domain name
- Following the domain is the path to the specific resource within that domain
