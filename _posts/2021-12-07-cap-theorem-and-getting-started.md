## Dev Setup

To start a project, install: Chrome, VS Code, npm, Node.js, Angular, TypeScript

## CAP Theorem

A distributed system can only guarantee **two** of:

- **Consistency**
- **Availability**
- **Partition Tolerance**

- **RDBMS** tends to focus on CA with ACID properties
- **NoSQL** DBs focus on AP or CP depending on the system
